What is LifeLink?

LifeLink is a centralized healthcare assistance system that connects citizens, hospitals, blood banks, ambulance services and donors under one digital roof. It tracks blood availability, organ availability, medicine stock, AI-based emergency triage, health monitoring, pill reminders and donor consent — all in a single platform.

Silent Guardian

LifeLink quietly monitors vitals, predicts emergencies before they erupt, and links the right life-saving resource to the right person at the right time.

Built for Real-World Constraints

Powered by a Flask backend, Firebase database, and a simple HTML/CSS/JS frontend, LifeLink is optimized for regions where medical access is unpredictable and infrastructure is limited.

Core Capabilities

A digital bridge between patients, hospitals and emergency services — designed to reduce response time and centralize life-saving information.

🔴 Blood & Organ Availability

Real-time snapshot of blood units, organ listings, hospital readiness and donor availability.

🧠 AI-Based Emergency Triage

Classifies case severity, suggests first-aid steps and notifies hospitals and ambulances instantly.

❤️ Health Monitoring & Prediction

Tracks vitals, detects anomalies and predicts possible emergencies for high-risk users.

⏰ Pill Reminders & Medicine Data

Pill schedules, refill alerts and medicine stock visibility across connected hospitals.

🎙️ Voice-Based ML Commands

Hands-free commands for searching blood, checking organ compatibility and triggering requests.

🫂 Donor & Consent Management

Secure donor profiles, encrypted consent logs and transparent history.

Our Vision

To ensure no patient waits for a blood unit, no organ goes unused, no ambulance arrives too late and no emergency is blind to data.
